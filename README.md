Ths repo contains the work done by me for my master thesis regarding the Continual Learning framework in Machine Learning under the lens of Infinite width neural networks.
In the specific the aspects that were taken into considerations are the dynamics of training of the network under 2 different parametrizations:

* Neural Tangent Parametrization (NTP)
* Maximum Update Parametrization ($\mu P$), also called Mean Field

This was done looking at the dynamics of the Neural Tangent Kernel and inspecting the impact of the a specific task training on the other tasks.
The DMFT.ipynb contains the simultation and the real experiment of a network under MUP
The NTK.ipynb collects as well simulations and real experiment of a NTP network
